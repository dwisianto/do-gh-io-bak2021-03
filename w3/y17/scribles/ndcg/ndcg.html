Normalized Discounted Cumulative Gain

Definition of the ranking metric

  • Binary Relevance : Precision@K (P@K), Mean Average Precision (MAP), and Mean Reciprocal Rank (MRR)
  • Multiple levels of relevance : Normalized Discounted Cumulative Gain (NDCG)
